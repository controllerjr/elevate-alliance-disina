Our Mission

The Disina Elevate Alliance Care Foundation is a community-focused initiative dedicated to empowering, educating, and supporting people, especially in rural and underserved areas. We believe in creating a ripple effect of positive change that transforms lives and strengthens communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the foundation? ▼

You can join by filling out the form on our "Join Us" page or by clicking the "Join Us" button in the navigation. We welcome all who want to help!

How do I donate? ▼

You can donate securely online through our donation page, which will be integrated with Paystack or Flutterwave. We also accept direct bank transfers.

Where does the foundation operate? ▼

We primarily focus our efforts on rural and underserved communities in Disina and surrounding areas. Our goal is to expand our reach as the foundation grows.
